We are also providing Pro-Am

It is one of the most effective and simple training system that implies that a beginner dances in tandem with a professional dancer, and under his guidance comprehends all the basics of dance. It makes it possible to train a dancer quickly enough, regardless of his age and initial level of physical fitness.

Why dance?

The body says what words cannot.

It’s a beauty in every single movement, art in every single breath, and expression of every hidden emotion.

  • Dancing increases confidence, social and communication skills as well as improve self-esteem and over all attentiveness in individuals.

  • The physical benefits of dancing include improved balance between muscles and fat, or overall body composition, improved muscular and overall strength.

  • Any form of exercise is great for relieving stress in the mind and body. Dance is no different. Dancing is a great stress reliever, not only because it is great physically for the body but it is also emotionally therapeutic.
